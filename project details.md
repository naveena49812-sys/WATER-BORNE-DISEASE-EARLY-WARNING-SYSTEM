# Water-Borne Disease Early Warning System

## 1. Project Title

Water-Borne Disease Early Warning System

## 2. Problem Statement

Water-borne diseases such as cholera, typhoid, and dysentery pose serious public health risks. Traditional monitoring methods often detect outbreaks only after they occur. An early warning system is needed to analyze water quality and environmental data, predict disease risks, and provide timely alerts for preventive action.

## 3. Project Objectives

* Monitor water quality parameters in real time.
* Detect potential disease outbreak risks at an early stage.
* Generate alerts for health authorities and communities.
* Improve public health through preventive measures.
* Reduce the spread of water-borne diseases.
